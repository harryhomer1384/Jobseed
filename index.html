Simple Process

Three Steps to Your First Job

From download to first paycheck, we've made it safe and simple.

1

Create Your Profile

Download the app, verify your age with a school ID, and build your profile with your interests, skills, and schedule. If you're under 18, add a parent or guardian.

2

Get Parent Approval

Your parent receives a consent request via email. They review safety features, set communication preferences, and approve your account. They stay in the loop on all applications.

3

Apply & Start Earning

Browse verified jobs matched to your profile. Apply with one tap, interview through safe in-app messaging, and start building your future. Your skills portfolio grows with every job.
